Flyers for the Boston College Acoustics

As Social Media and Marketing Coordinator for the Boston College Acoustics, it is my responsibility to create posters for the group's various perfomances and other events. For each poster, I use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to design them. These programs alow me to create intriguing digital graphics to catch our audience's eye and call them to attend our events. All of our performances have a theme, so I cater the poster to the theme and find creative ways to carry it through our other promotional content.
