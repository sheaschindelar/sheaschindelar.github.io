Can Art Make A Difference?

This was my final project for Introduction to Photoshop and Illustrator. The prompt asked for us to question what the role of art was in the world -- how can it make a difference? What groups can be influenced? What makes it most effective? I chose to answer this prompt with a topic that was meaningful to me: mental health in high school students. High school can be a difficult part of life to navigate through, and many students have unhealthy was of coping with the diffiiculties of trying to make friends, get good grades, and fitting in. I took inspiration from the "Got Milk" campaign from the nineties and early two-thousands. I paired this theme with shocking statistics and equally as shocking and creepy graphics to create impactful messaging.
