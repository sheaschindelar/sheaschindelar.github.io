Altered History

This was my a project for Introduction to Photoshop and Illustrator. The prompt asked for us to question what would be different if significant events happened in another way, or didn't even happen at all. We had to change history using Photoshop and the skills we were learning in class, like masks, adjustment layers, and adding grain. As someone that loves to watch movies and TV shows, I chose to reimagine the casting of classic movies. Many movies had multiple big name (sometimes future big name) actors and actresses audition for an iconic role, and I was curious as to how that would change modern celebrity and media culture. To show this, I edited other actors and actresses that were considered for a role onto a movie's poster.
