Website Design

When designing websites, I have a very specific and organized process. I use tools from the Adobe create suite like Illustrator and Photoshop to create intriguing graphics, and Lightroom to bring images to life. I also use Procreate plan sites and digitize illustrations.

With clients, my main goal is to create a site that matches their needs as well as their brand and identity. I begin this process by consulting with clients and finding out what they are looking for. I dive into who they are as a company or group and develop a concept for their site and move on to building it. If needed, I will go onsite and take pictures for the website, like I did with Saxton Falls Sand and Gravel. Throughout the entire process, I consult with the client to ensure I am meeting their vision.
